Museum of Candy Website
This is a simple website that showcases the Museum of Candy, a fictional place where you can explore the history and culture of candy. The website features a colorful and attractive design, with images and text that describe the museum’s exhibits and attractions. The website also uses responsive media queries to adapt to different screen sizes and devices, ensuring a smooth and user-friendly experience.

Features
A landing page with a full-screen background image and a navigation bar
A section with three cards that highlight the main features of the museum: the candy wall, the candy art, and the candy store
A section with a gallery of images that showcase the museum’s exhibits and attractions
A section with a contact form and a map that show the museum’s location and opening hours
A footer with social media icons and links
Technologies
HTML5
CSS3
Bootstrap 4
